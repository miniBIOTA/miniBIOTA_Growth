# Commercial Operating Model

## Purpose
- Define how miniBIOTA turns mission, technical credibility, and public interest into durable business support.
- Keep current-state commercial signals separate from future-state commercial design.

## Current Signals Already Present In The Vault
- The company-level task list already calls for a business plan, a media kit, a sponsorship package, and speaking/pitching activity.
- The brand layer already frames miniBIOTA as a project that can attract audiences, partners, donors, and media attention.
- The website strategy and information architecture already expose public conversion surfaces through `/donate`, `/events`, `/build`, and newsletter signup routes.
- The web platform already captures newsletter signups into `email_signups`, but the external provider sync is still an open operational step.

## Current Commercial Surfaces
- `/donate` gives the public web layer a direct support surface.
- `/events` gives the project a place to support speaking, appearances, and public activations.
- `/build` is positioned for affiliate-backed hardware and resource content.
- `/subscribe` and `/signup` already act as audience-capture paths for newsletter growth.

## Planned Near-Term Revenue And Partnership Lanes
- Sponsorships and brand partnerships built around the biosphere story, engineering visibility, and public-facing documentation.
- Speaking and event appearances that translate the founder story, ecological logic, and technical build into partner-facing opportunities.
- Audience-supported revenue paths such as donation, Patreon-adjacent membership flow, and newsletter-driven conversion.
- Affiliate-backed build resources where the hardware journey itself can support a monetization-adjacent content surface.

## Core Dependencies
- A real business plan is still needed so commercial outreach is tied to operating reality rather than generic pitch language.
- Brand messaging needs to be strong enough to explain why miniBIOTA matters to sponsors, hosts, donors, and collaborators.
- Content proof is needed so outreach has credible artifacts, visuals, and system progress to point at.
- A lightweight relationship pipeline is needed so commercial follow-through is not lost across chats and scattered notes.

## Current Unknowns
- No pricing model, sponsorship tiers, or partner package structure is yet documented.
- No standard owner, cadence, or CRM-style workflow is yet defined for this function.
- No explicit rules yet define how email capture, website conversion, and outbound partner follow-up connect operationally.
